 Smart City

Blockchain Driven IoT Soltutions for Smart Ctities of the Future

Smart Cities of the future should structure their end-goals across carefully crafted set of strategic pillars. 

Efficiency

Today's cities operate with legacy processes and systems. In the last century, there were a lot of constraints limiting the ability to change to being more agile. This is no longer the case now with the advancement of crypto and blockchain technologies.

Seamless Integration

Quantum-Computing technologies can provide the capabilities for realtime integration in a highly secure environment. Especially utilizing the latest and greatest cryptographic techniques developed recently with the emergence of blockchain paradigms.

Safe and Privacy

Security was and continues to be essential. We have seen a lot of major breaches in the last months, this demonstrates how outdated the technologies utilized in the public as well as in private areas are. Blockchain , Smart Contracts Ethereum, and IPFS Cryptography solutions can me a perfect enabler to move in the right direction.

For cities to thrive in the next age, they should play to seek global sustainability and competitiveness. the city leadership has to streamline their initiatives into a unified strategy, which in turn is prioritized into focal areas for a significant collective gain and to provide the foundation for innovation.

Economical Impace

Innovative economiec conditions feeling entrepreneurshiop and global competitiveness

Proper Governance

Innovative, transparent government services with public, private and civil engagement

People Needs

Culture of continuous learning, innoovating and participating in an inclusive society

Innovation

Exceptional quality of life, accessible education and healthcare. Culturally vibrant lifestyle.

Mobility

Seamless, efficient transport  of people and movement of ideas enabled by innovative infrastructures.

Environment

Sustainable technologies for resources, pollution and asset management for sustainability.

Geared for success

Key Success Factors

In order to be successful, cities need to have the following items addressed...

Identity
Implementing identity patterns across all contexts: physical web, virtual services, and user realm.
Security
Advancements in blockchain and cryptography means we can now enable use cases that were not even possible recently. this is a new ventured land of opportunity.
Privary
Real time, secure access to domain-wide data allowing real-time intelligence to making real-time decisions. All of this while masking identity and preserving privacy.
